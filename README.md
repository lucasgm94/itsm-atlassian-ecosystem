# 🛠️ IT Service Management (ITSM) & Help Desk Labs

### Project Overview
This repository focuses on professional **IT Support Operations** and **Service Management**. It simulates real-world scenarios for incident resolution, technical documentation, and standardized workflows to ensure high-quality IT service delivery.

---

### 🚀 Core Competencies

#### 1. Incident & Request Management
* **Lifecycle Management:** From ticket creation to final resolution and post-mortem analysis.
* **SLA Compliance:** Prioritizing tasks based on business impact and urgency levels.
* **Process Automation:** Streamlining repetitive tasks to improve Resolution Time (MTTR).

#### 2. Technical Documentation (Knowledge Base)
* Creating **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)** for hardware and software troubleshooting.
* Building self-service articles to empower users and reduce ticket volume.
* Version control for IT infrastructure documentation.

#### 3. Service Delivery & Infrastructure Support
* **Hardware/Software Lifecycle:** Onboarding, provisioning, and inventory management.
* **Remote & Field Support:** Best practices for troubleshooting in hybrid work environments.

---

### 🛠️ Platforms & Methodologies
* **ITSM Frameworks:** ITIL-aligned workflows and best practices.
* **Collaboration Tools:** Jira Service Management, Trello, Confluence, and Slack.
* **Technical Writing:** Markdown-based documentation.
